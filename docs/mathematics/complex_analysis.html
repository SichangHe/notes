Complex Analysis

complex number C

z=(x,y) in complex plane where x,y∈R

  • real part, x=Rez
  • imaginary part, y=Imz

complex plane

  • real axis/ imaginary axis
  • (0,y) pure imaginary number, y=0

binary operation of complex number

  • addition, (x1​,y1​)+(x2​,y2​)=(x1​+x2​,y1​+y2​)
  • multiplication, (x1​,y1​)⋅(x2​,y2​)=(x1​x2​−y1​y2​,x1​y2​+x2​y1​)

complex number short syntax

(x,y)=(x,0)+(0,y)=(x,0)+(0,1)⋅(y,0)=:x+iy

where i:=(0,1)
